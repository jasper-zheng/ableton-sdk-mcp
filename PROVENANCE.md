# Provenance & credits

This repository's **knowledge skills** (`.claude/skills/`) were distilled from two
copyrighted sources. Only **distilled, derivative notes** (parameter tables, technique
summaries, paraphrased strategies) are included here — the original works are **not**
redistributed.

This repository is a research prototype with no formal license yet.

## Sources
- **Ableton Live 12 Manual** (`live12-manual-en.pdf`) — © Ableton AG. Used to distil
  device/parameter references and feature concepts (skills: `ableton-midi`,
  `ableton-sound-design`, `ableton-arrangement`, `ableton-mixing`, `ableton-warping`).
- **Dennis DeSantis, *Making Music: 74 Creative Strategies for Electronic Music
  Producers*** (© Ableton AG, 2015) — used to distil the creative strategies (skill:
  `music-strategies`).

Both PDFs are excluded from this package.
